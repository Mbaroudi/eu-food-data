Approved red, poultry, and game meat establishments (Updated July 2014)

In this section you will find lists of meat establishments that are approved to slaughter and/or cut meat.

These are as follows:

  • domestic ungulates: cattle, calves, sheep, goats, pigs, bison, water buffalo and horses
  • poultry: farmed birds (chicken, turkey, duck, geese, guinea fowl, quail)
  • lagomorphs: rabbits, hares and rodents
  • farmed game: farmed deer, farmed boar and ratites (flightless birds: e.g. ostrich, rhea, emu)
  • wild game (includes wild birds, wild lagomorphs, wild ungulates and wild land mammals)

The meat establishments listed below are subject to Regulations (EC) 852/2004 and (EC) 853/2004 and must be approved by the Food Standards Agency (FSA), in order to operate, unless they meet the exemption criteria in Regulation (EC) 853/2004:

  • Slaughterhouses
  • Farm slaughter facilities
  • Game Handling establishments
  • Cutting plants

These establishments are subject to official controls enforced and executed by the FSA.

When co-located with an approved slaughterhouse, cutting plant or Game Handling establishment, the following associated activities are also approved by the FSA (when stand-alone, they are approved by the local authority):

  • Minced meat, meat preparations and mechanically separated meat establishments
  • Processing plants (meat products, rendered animal fats and greaves, treated stomach, bladders, intestines, gelatine, collagen)
  • Cold stores
  • Re-wrapping establishments

List of meat establishments in the UK

These meat establishments are operating under Regulation (EC) No 852/2004, Regulation (EC) No 853/2004 and Regulation (EC) No 882/2004 and are either conditionally approved or approved.

Please note that:

  • the former Farmed Game Handling Facilities are now known as On Farm Slaughter Facilities
  • the former Farmed Game Processing Facilities are now known as Slaughterhouses the activities of which are limited to the dressing of carcasses
  • the former Wild Game Processing Facilities are now known as Game Handling Establishments
  • the coldstores and re-wrapping centres listed are on the same site as a slaughterhouse, cutting plant or game handling establishment.
